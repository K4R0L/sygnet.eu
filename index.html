_Welcome to Sygnet.eu

Sygnet.eu is a smart cryptographic tool created by Tokenika

It will enable you to use your banking account as a means to bootstrap your digital identity.
We aim to make the process of proving who you are on the Internet as simple as authorizing a bank transfer.

To reach this goal we take advantage of the forthcoming European PSD2 legislation.
Initially our system will be geared towards KYC - but that's just the first step.
Ultimately we want to offer a whole range of digital identity services leveraging the power of EOS infrastructure.
